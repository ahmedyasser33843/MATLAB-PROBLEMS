A.01
Evaluate 3 + 4 * 2 - (6 / 3)^2 step by step in MATLAB and predict the result before running it. What does ans contain after each line?

A.06
MATLAB is case-sensitive. Predict the outputs of: A=5; a=3; B=A+a; b=a-A. Now reassign a = A*2 and recompute B. Does B change? Why or why not?
In MATLAB, variables are not linked. Reassigning a does not retroactively change B — B was computed at the moment of assignment and holds that value until explicitly recomputed.
